Chapter 1: The Riddle of the Hieroglyphs

As you enter the construction site of the pyramids, you come across a wall adorned with hieroglyphs. One inscription catches your eye:

"In the heart of the desert, we rise,
Towers of stone that touch the skies.
Built with labor and sweat, not gold,
Our secrets hidden, yet to be told.

What stone was used to construct the pyramids,
Quarried from the earth where the river flows?
Its name you must uncover, if you are wise."

Puzzle

Your task is to solve the riddle of the Hieroglyphs to unlock the next clue and continue your journey.

Hint: Think about the materials used in the construction of the Egyptian pyramids, particularly the type of stone that was quarried near the Nile River.
